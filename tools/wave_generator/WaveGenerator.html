WaveTable Generator

This is an online tool to generate new wave forms for Arduino. Simply draw in the drawing area and the hit the GENERATE button to generate the new wave table.



Generates the wave table. Scale to maximum volume.
To upload the wave table to your arduino sketch, click in the area to the left, press "Ctrl-A" (select all) in your browser. Then copy everything into "table.h" from the synth sketch and upload.
